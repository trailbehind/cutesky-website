Cute Sky

iPhone Weather and iPad Weather with NOAA Radar and Wunderground

Cute Sky will make you smile, and give you useful weather info.

Cute Sky integrates the best weather data from The Weather Channel's Wunderground service, with critical weather alerts from NOAA, and beautiful pictures from Flickr.

Cute Pics

Cute Sky is free of charge and free of 3rd-party ads.

You can support us by purchasing the "Cute Pics" in-app purchase, which lets you choose to show pictures of doggies, kitties, and babies, instead of just weather scenery.

Features

  • view current weather info and forecasts
  
  • view NEXRAD radar on the map
  
  • use Cute Sky on any iPhone, iPad, or iPod
  
  • mark favorites and remember your last recent places
  
  • uncluttered, button-free design that showcases beautiful photos
